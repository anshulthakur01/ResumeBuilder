Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ResumeBuilder

Is ResumeBuilder really free?

Yes! ResumeBuilder offers a free plan with access to 5+ professional templates and basic features. We also offer premium plans with advanced features like AI optimization, unlimited templates, and PDF downloads.

How do I download my resume?

Once you've finished creating your resume, simply click the "Download" button. Free users can download as a text file, while premium users can download as a high-quality PDF with preserved formatting.

Are your templates ATS-friendly?

Absolutely! All our templates are designed to pass through Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) used by most employers. We follow best practices for ATS compatibility including clean formatting, standard fonts, and proper section headings.

Can I switch templates after creating my resume?

Yes! One of our key features is the ability to switch templates at any time without losing your content. This allows you to experiment with different designs for different applications.
